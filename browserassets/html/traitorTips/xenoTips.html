You are an Xenomorph!

1. Your goal is to serve your Queen, spread weeds, and infect the all of the survivors on the station.

2. Xenomorphic aliens are split into two main types and five subcastes. Drones have the very important job of planting weeds, secreting resin for building, and building resin structures. Weeds slow down humans significantly, making it difficult for them to invade your hive. Sentinels, who evolve from Drones, are tasked with defending the hive, and have better stats which help them do so. Warriors are a very powerful caste which is expected to expand the hive by infecting humans. They evolve from Sentinels. Praetorians are the extremely durable guardians of the hive and the Queen, and also evolve from Sentinels.The Queen is the autocratic ruler of the hive, whose will cannot be disobeyed. She evolves from a Praetorian.

Xenomorphs don't need to breathe, so they can enter space without a spacesuit or internals.
