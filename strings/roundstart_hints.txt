You can suplex most aliens. Not Queens or Praetorians, though. Do this by grabbing them and emoting *flip.
Aliens have the following emotes: *roar, *snarl, and *hiss.
Aliens can eat monkeys or other critters for an extra plasma boost, plus faster healing for a bit.
If you scream on the station, chances are that someone, or something, heard it.
Attacking a monkey in a group of monkeys is a generally a bad idea.
Items that involve fire are good for driving away Xenomorphs.
All aliens except Queens and Praetorians can ventcrawl.
Aliens can eat the remains of their host(gibs and organs) for extra plasma. This transfers to their next evolution as well.
